Privacy Policy

I want to be transparent about your privacy when using the PFStracker. Since you ended up on this page, that means you must be at least a little bit interested about your own privacy.

So what does the PFStracker do that could relate to your privacy?

  • I store your email address that you use for logging in, but also for recovering your password if you ever lose it. I will not email you about updates, marketing and the likes. All you will ever get from PFStracker is automated recovery emails.
  • The following information is public in the PFStracker, anyone can search for these:
    • PFS-number
    • Displayname
    • Your played history
    • Your characters (only if you turn 'public characters' on in your settings)
  • I do not know the password you use for PFStracker. The passwords are stored in the database as a hash, a one way process. For techies: I use a salt for each account and use a form of bcrypt. The PFStracker code is available on github.
  • I run no Google Analytics or other ways to track you. There are no third party tracking cookies used on this site, only cookies to ensure the site works as intended. You will not be tracked.
  • I host the PFStracker in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. I run my own server, the hosting party cannot login to my server.
  • Due to being hosted in Amsterdam I am subject to european law and try to adhere to all the legislation as written down in the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
  • Traffic in transit is encrypted between your browser and the server.
